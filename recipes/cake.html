Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

To create this delicious recipe you will need:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Baking soda
  • Oil
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Whisk ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Divide batter evenly between pans..
  4. Bake for 1 hour.
  5. Make frosting.
  6. Frost cake.
